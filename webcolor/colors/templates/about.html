About SnappyColor

Animal Randomizer (Random Animal Generator) is an online random animal generator tool used to generate any random animal's information by animal category.

Users can generate only four random animal details at a time.

We have designed this online random animal generator tool to provide information about animals that can be useful for educational purposes, general knowledge about Animals, For Kids, Fun purposes, Curiosity, etc.

If I talk about animal information, Then it could be like animal name, animal scientific name, animal category, lifespan, some facts, pictures, and some other information. You can also download the animal information in your system just by clicking the Download Animal Info button.

To generate the random animal details, Click Here to visit the tool page and choose the animal category you want to generate also choose any number between upto 4 and then click on the Generate Random Animal button, and you will get redirected to another page where you will see animal details of category that you had selected. You can download the animal information as well.
